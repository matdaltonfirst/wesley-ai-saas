Connect your YouTube channel

Wesley will ingest your recent sermons and check daily for new ones, so visitors can ask "What was Sunday's message about?" Long videos take a few minutes each — it runs in the background.

When a website visitor shares their contact info with Wesley, they'll be added to Planning Center People automatically — with a note about what they asked — so your team can follow up. You'll sign in on Planning Center's site; Wesley never sees your password.
